Dominique DeMoe

The fun stuff

My name is Dominique DeMoe and I am a designer and developer. In my free time, I can be found learning new things (currently teaching myself French), exploring Philadelphia (you'll find me at a museum or a good restaurant) and spending time with my family.

My elevator pitch

Data visualization specialist with a certificate in data visualization and analytics from the University of Pennsylvania. A keen eye for design, with the ability to quickly adapt to new environments and skilled in problem solving. Proven technical and leadership abilities in web data visualization and management environments as exemplified by my experience working with senior leadership to offer judgement and advice on daily projects. Well versed in presenting ideas to staff and driving collaboration across multifunctional teams. Experienced in Python, SQL and data visualization tools such as JavaScript and D3.js. With my three years of experience in data visualization, I understand the importance of and am passionate about equal access to relevant and truthful information. My technical skills and ability to communicate with an audience allow me to help reach a wide variety of people.
