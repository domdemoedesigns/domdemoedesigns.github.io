Rules


Match cards to see images of different components of the Solar System. Click on two cards to try to make a match, if they show the same image, they will stay turned. If not, they will flip back. You can only flip two cards at once and will have to wait until they flip back to try another card.


When you have matched all of the cards, click the button at the bottom of the page that says the name of the level (The Planets, The Moons, or Asteroids, Comets and Meteors) to learn the names of each image. You can also click "Play Again" to reset the board, choose another level or click "Learn More" to read more information about the Solar System.
