Problem

There are many popular typefaces but if people don't look past the Helvetica's and Baskerville's of the world, no one will ever use other typefaces that are just as good.


Solution

Instead of looking to a typeface that had a significant impact on the world, I looked at type designers who have had significant impacts on the design world and decided to create a type specification book on one of their lesser known typefaces.



Ideation

My chosen typeface was Benton Sans created by both Tobias Frere-Jones and Cyrus Highsmith. Benton Sans is known for being the typeface of the Martha Stewart Living magazine and website as well as the Marvel Logo.


I found that Benton Sans is also used as the typeface for the Lonely Planet's travel books and decided to create a type spec book inspired by the Lonely Planet.
